# Event Information

Hello! We cordially invite you to participate in the "NVIDIA National Taiwan University Campus Ambassador and Education Resources Briefing." This briefing aims to provide a deeper understanding of the NVIDIA Deep Learning Institute (DLI) Ambassador Program and the educational resources it offers. In the rapidly evolving field of artificial intelligence, providing students with cutting-edge, practical knowledge is our shared mission as educators.
