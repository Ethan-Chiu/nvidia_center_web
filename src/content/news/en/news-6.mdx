# Event Information

[NVIDIA GTC 2025](https://www.nvidia.com/gtc/) is coming back to San Jose, California, on March 17–20. This premier
event brings together the world’s leading developers, researchers, engineers, business
leaders, and innovators from various industries to share their work, cultivate new skills, and
network with fellow experts.

<br/>

We're excited to announce that the [call for submissions](https://www.nvidia.com/gtc/call-for-submissions/) is now open. We welcome you to
submit a talk, poster, or tutorial proposal on a topic of your choice. Accepted sessions will
be delivered in person in San Jose and recorded for our online global audience. GTC 2024
attracted 300,000 registrants this past Spring, with 19,000 in-person attendees. GTC 2025
is expected to be the biggest and most exciting event yet.

<br/>

With an increasing number of submissions each year, we recommend checking out our
[How-To Tips page](https://register.nvidia.com/flow/nvidia/gtcs25/cfsresources/page/howtosubmit) for valuable guidance on writing a great GTC title and description.

Submit your talk proposal for review [here](https://register.nvidia.com/flow/nvidia/gtcs25/cfsmainex).
Poster submissions can be submitted [here](https://www.nvidia.com/auth/gtc?scope=openid+email+profile&client_id=FNwji43RhQ7B2YKM7B6rC6N7KA_Gu3-ohkaoljY9NJ8&redirect_uri=https%3A%2F%2Fevents.rainfocus.com%2Foauth%2Fnvidia%2F1629402589906001W3aJ&response_type=code&state=98e854cab3bb4bb1bb5c39d15f40af82be6f7574c6c3e464d67ddbdcea3dccfd1f4ebc15e6fe34d6cf7053d2244b7cbeea6ecd&from=gtcs25-cfsposters).

# Deadline
The deadline for talk submissions is **September 12, 2024, at 5 p.m. PT** and poster
submissions is **October 3, 2024 at 5 p.m. PT**.
