## About the NVIDIA Academic Grant Program

The aim of the NVIDIA Academic Grant Program is to provide computational support to the academic community and assist research teams in advancing innovative research. Selected proposals will receive the following resource support based on research needs:
- Jetson Orin Nano Development Kit
- Jetson AGX Orin Development Kit
- NVIDIA RTX 6000 Ada
- Up to 32,000 hours of A100 40GB computing time or equivalent resources

## Scope of Applications

The research areas covered by this program include but are not limited to:
- GenAI & LLMs (Scientific Applications, Multi-modality, Train/Customize/Accelerate)
- Simulation and Modeling (Quantum Computing, Scientific Simulation, CFD, Physics-informed ML)
- Edge AI (6G, Federated Learning, Smart Spaces, Autonomous Driving, Robotics)
- Data Science (Processing, Route Optimization, Statistical Methods, Graph Analytics, GNNs)
- Graphics and Vision (AR/VR, Ray Tracing, Rendering, AI for Graphics, Simulation)

## Application Notes
- Each faculty member is limited to submitting one proposal per year.
- Proposal submission period: October 1, 2024, to December 31, 2024
- Announcement of results: March 2025
- Expected hardware shipping date: May 2025
- GPU usage period: April 1, 2025, to September 30, 2025

This program aims to support innovative research, and NVIDIA does not claim ownership of the intellectual property (IP) developed through this program.
