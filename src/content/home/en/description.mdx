The **NVIDIA-NTU Artificial Intelligence Joint Innovation Center** was established in 2023 as a significant outcome of the collaboration between NVIDIA and National Taiwan University. Its aim is to promote the development and application of artificial intelligence technology in Taiwan. With the rapid advancement of artificial intelligence technology, the Taiwanese government encourages industry-academic cooperation, and the partnership between NVIDIA and National Taiwan University will contribute to the growth of Taiwan's artificial intelligence industry. The center is dedicated to accelerating interdisciplinary research, technology development, and educational outreach in artificial intelligence by leveraging the resources of both NVIDIA and the university.
